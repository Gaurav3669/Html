City Palace

The City Palace, Jaipur is a royal residence and former administrative headquarters of the rulers of the Jaipur State1. It was constructed between 1729 and 1732 under the reign of Maharaja Sawai Jai Singh II, who moved his court to Jaipur from Amber due to an increase in population and water shortage problems2. The palace was built by Raja Man Singh3. It is located in the heart of the Pink City Jaipur and is a testament to the rich culture and heritage of the state2.
